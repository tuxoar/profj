## Approach

My pedagogy grows directly out of my research: I develop materials for Chinese
learners of various levels with the goal of transforming students into lifelong,
self-motivated, and effective learners. Drawing on the **performed-culture**
approach, I build courses around purposeful action in authentic cultural
contexts, so that learners rehearse real roles rather than memorize
decontextualized forms.
