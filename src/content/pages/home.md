## About

I am an Associate Professor of Chinese at Hamilton College, where I have taught
since 2017 in the Department of East Asian Languages & Literatures and the
programs in Chinese and Asian Studies. My work sits at the meeting point of
**applied linguistics** and **language pedagogy**: I research the psychology of
second-language motivation and translate what I find into materials and
classrooms that help students perform beyond proficiency.

My teaching and research focus on understanding sustainable foreign-language
learning motivation and on creating motivating experiences in the classroom and
beyond — with the goal of transforming students into lifelong, self-motivated,
and effective learners.
